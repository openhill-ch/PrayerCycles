# Privacy Policy for Prayer Cycles

_Last updated: [DATE]_

Prayer Cycles ("the app") is designed to be private by default. This policy
explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

## The short version

**Prayer Cycles does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of your data.**
Everything you enter — prayer lists, prayers, descriptions, tags, and history —
stays on your device. There are no accounts, no servers, no analytics, and no
third-party tracking.

## What data the app stores

The app stores the following **locally on your device only**:

- Prayer lists and their settings (cadence, schedule, tags)
- Individual prayers, including titles, descriptions, and tags
- Your prayer history (which prayers you've prayed and when)
- App preferences (theme, language, sound, and similar settings)

This information is saved in your device's local storage. It is **encrypted at
rest** using a key stored in your device's secure storage (Apple Keychain on
iOS). The app cannot read your data without that key, and the key never leaves
your device except through Apple's own iCloud Keychain sync, if you have that
enabled.

## What the app does NOT do

- It does **not** send your data to any server.
- It does **not** use analytics, advertising, or tracking tools.
- It does **not** require an account or any personal information.
- It does **not** share your data with third parties.

## Bible verse feature

The app includes a King James Version (KJV) Bible text bundled inside the app
for the verse-autocomplete feature. Looking up a verse happens entirely on your
device — no network request is made, and your searches are not recorded or sent
anywhere.

## Backups

You can export your data to a backup file from within the app. This file is
created on your device and is yours to keep. The app does not upload it
anywhere. Where you choose to store that backup file (for example, in iCloud
Drive or another service) is governed by that service's own privacy policy, not
this one.

## Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone,
including children.
